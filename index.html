how it works

More economy and organization for your kitchen

Starting from the principle that today the market of gastronomy software is focused on the fiscal part of the restaurant, we perceive an opportunity to develop something geared specifically for the kitchen. PlanChef Kitchen Management is a simple system that adds more organization, economy and technical qualification to the area of ​​gastronomy, thereby simplifying kitchen management.
